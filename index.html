About Us

Imagine a scenario where you want to travel somewhere for a few hours and you need a vehicle for that , you don't own one and obviously you don't want to spend an ample amount of money in buying a bike just to use it for a couple of hours. Also booking a cab or a bike online is costing too much.
Wouldn't it be the best if someone who own a bike and is not using it and lives nearby lend his/her bike to you and charges a resonable amount that you can afford?
We will be designing a project that will do exactly the same for you. We will provide an interface where you can register as a :

  • lessor(the one who owns the resource)
  • lessee(the one who want to rent it)
Being a lessor you can provide your bike number, time duration for which you want to rent it, your location, a minimum amount that you would charge and some images of your vehicle.
If you register as a lessee you can select a location where you are at and you will get a list of vehicles available at your location for renting. You can request to book whichever vehicle you like the best and if the owner accepts your request you both can contact each other and proceed further.
This way our application encourages resource utilisation and will also help you get in touch with new people in your locality and help one another.
